About Us

Our story begins with OKcupid. Rachel found me and we had an instant connection. We texted non-stop for a week and then decided to meet up. Our first date was at Lazy Dog Restaurant and then we went to go see The Visit. After the movie we talked for hours until Starbucks closed at like 2am. We had another date the next day and since then we've pretty much been inseperable.

The Proposal

This May Rachel and I went on a roadtrip of the Pacifc Northwest. Little did I know there was an ulterior motive for this trip. We visited Ashland, Portland, Seattle, and Spokane. While the whole trip was amazing my favorite place were Seattle and Spokane. In Spokane we stayed at the beautiful Historic Davenport Hotel. Rachel took me to this gorgeous winery called Arbor Crest, where she very nervously and a little bit awkwardly proposed to me. She had it all planned out where I was wearing a purple dress and she was wearing kahki pants and a blue vest. Outfits similar to what Flynn Rider and Rapunzle wear in Tangled. The ring is gorgeous and Tangled themed as well. That night she took me to a lantern fest and we got to set off a latern to celebrate our engagement.
